About Iryna

I am a Marriage and Family therapist registered intern practicing under supervision of Don Hadlock, licensed MFT (license #) in Campbell.

I have graduated from Institute of Transpersonal Psychology in 2014 with a Master's in Counseling Psychology and have been working with children and their families since then. I use a direct, experiential, process-oriented approach in my work and combine it with Jungian, Humanistic, and Cognitive-Behavioral techniques.

I have experience of working with depression, psychosis, anxiety, trauma, and spirituality-related issues.
